Device Types

  • FreeRun: When power is applied, both the horn and strobe will run.
  • Selectable: When power is applied, the horn will operate normally. Strobe operation is controlled by a switch next to the wiring terminals, allowing the strobe to operate in SmartSync or Freerun mode.
  • Syncable: Horn will operate when power is applied, but strobe will only flash when power is removed. Enabling a non-continuous code from a panel will achieve this.
  • SmartSync: Requires a SmartSync-compatible Simplex panel or power supply (4006, 4008, 4010, 4009 IDNet NAC Extender) or a 4905-9938 SmartSync Module to operate.
  • Addressable: Requires a TrueNAC-compatible Simplex panel or power supply (4100U or 4100ES with TrueAlert Power Supply, any IDNAC power supply, or 4009 TrueAlert Addressable Controller) to operate.
  • ES: Requires an IDNAC-compatible Simplex panel or power supply (IDNAC 4007ES, IDNAC 4010ES, 4100ES with EPS or ES-PS Power Supply [With IDNAC Card]) to operate. *Some* horn strobe models can be operated with a 4009 TrueAlert Addressable Controller.
***Remember never to apply Full Wave Rectified (FWR) to a Simplex Horn/Strobe! Filtered DC is critical!***
